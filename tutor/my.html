Biography

He has Bachelor in Science, Major in Computer Science (2008) and post-graduate certificate in IT Management (2010) by Devry Brazil. He also has MBA in Project and Business Management (2011) from Faculdade Ateneu and a Master in Science, Major Applied Computer Science from the University of Fortaleza (2015). Currently he is a PhD student at the University of Regina and a paper reviewer of the PMI Conferences. He has 9 years with experience in project management (PMP) and PHP development, 7 years as a teacher and 14 years in total in I.T.
